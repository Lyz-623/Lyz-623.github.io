About

Research

Research Interests

Optical Microscopy

Designing and using microscopy systems to capture rich spatial and spectral information from biological samples.

Computational Reconstruction

Combining optics, signal processing, and learning-based methods to improve image quality and extract quantitative measurements.

Biomedical Imaging Analysis

Applying hyperspectral and microscopic image analysis to pathology and tissue-level biomedical questions.
