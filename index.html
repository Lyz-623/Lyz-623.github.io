I am a master's student in Electronic Science and Technology at SUSTech, with a background in Biomedical Engineering. My research focuses on optical microscopy systems and computational methods for biomedical imaging, especially super-resolution fluorescence microscopy and Raman microscopy.

I am interested in building microscopy workflows that combine optical instrumentation, signal processing, and learning-based reconstruction to improve image stability, throughput, and data quality.

I am expected to begin a Ph.D. program in Physics at Politecnico di Milano in September 2026, working on two-dimensional electronic spectroscopy (2DES) under the supervision of Prof. Giulio Cerullo and Prof. Dario Polli.

Education

  • M.S. in Electronic Science and Technology, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), Shenzhen, China. September 2023 - expected July 2026.
  • B.S. in Biomedical Engineering, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), Shenzhen, China. September 2019 - June 2023.

Research Interests

  • Two-dimensional electronic spectroscopy (2DES) and ultrafast optical spectroscopy
  • Super-resolution fluorescence microscopy and single-molecule localization microscopy (SMLM)
  • Raman microscopy systems for high-throughput, label-free chemical imaging
  • Optical path alignment, focus stabilization, and drift correction for microscopy platforms
  • Deep learning-based spectral denoising, reconstruction, and unmixing
