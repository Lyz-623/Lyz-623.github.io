Yunze Li

Google Scholar GitHub OpenAlex

Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), Shenzhen, China

I work on microscopy, hyperspectral imaging, and computational methods for biomedical image analysis. My interests center on optical measurement, data-driven reconstruction, and microscopy workflows that make biological structure easier to observe, quantify, and understand.

This homepage keeps a concise record of my research interests, publications, and academic links.

Research Interests

  • Optical microscopy and biomedical imaging
  • Hyperspectral pathological imaging
  • Computational reconstruction and image analysis
  • Deep learning methods for microscopy data
